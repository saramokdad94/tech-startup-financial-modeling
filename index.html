My Services

  • Track key success metrics: Keep a close eye on the financial health of your startup by consistently tracking core metrics like revenue, costs, margins, and pipeline.
  • Build custom KPIs: We can build tailored KPIs—such as measuring product breadth per user—when standard metrics aren’t enough.
  • Financial forecasting: Forecasting key metrics based on historical data helps you make informed decisions and guide your startup’s growth with intention.
  • Data driven analysis: Let the data speak. Whether it’s a sensitivity analysis or scoring model, data can offer clarity and direction.

My Process

  1. Requirement Gathering: Define business objectives, key metrics, and data. I can guide you if you're unsure.
  2. Model Development: I build a prototype using dummy data to simulate expected outputs with forecasts, sensitivity analyses, and KPIs.
  3. Validation & Refinement: We review outputs together and make refinements based on your feedback.
  4. Handover & Training: I provide easy instructions to run the model, with optional support for refinements and insights.
